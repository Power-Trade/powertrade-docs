# BNB Options

Trade BNB options on PowerTrade with USDC settlement, flexible expiries, and defined risk.

## Why Trade BNB Options?

BNB options let traders express directional views, hedge spot exposure, and structure volatility strategies without taking linear spot risk.

## What You Can Do with BNB Options

- Buy BNB calls for upside exposure with capped downside (premium paid)
- Buy BNB puts to hedge downside risk in BNB holdings
- Build multi-leg structures (for example, spreads) to define risk/reward
- Trade event-driven volatility with short-dated expiries

## Contract Basics

- **Style:** European
- **Settlement:** USDC cash-settled
- **Expiry range:** Daily to longer-dated maturities (subject to listing schedule)

## Risk Reminder

Options can expire worthless. Always size positions appropriately and review max loss before placing a trade.
