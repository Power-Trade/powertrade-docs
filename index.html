📈 Trading on PowerTrade

Options Trading

Crypto options are financial derivatives that give traders the right, but not the obligation, to buy or sell an underlying cryptocurrency at a fixed price (the strike price) on or before a specific date.

📈 Call Options

Give you the right to buy cryptocurrency at the strike price.

  • Buy calls when you expect prices to rise
  • Unlimited profit potential
  • Example: BTC call at $50k, if BTC hits $55k, profit $5k minus premium

📉 Put Options

Give you the right to sell cryptocurrency at the strike price.

  • Buy puts when you expect prices to fall
  • Perfect for hedging downside risk
  • Example: ETH put at $3k, if ETH drops to $2.5k, profit $500 minus premium

Why Trade Options on PowerTrade?

  • European-style options: Can only be exercised at expiration, preventing early exercise risk
  • USDC cash-settled: No need to hold the underlying cryptocurrency
  • 80+ altcoin markets: The widest coverage in crypto
  • Tight pricing & deep liquidity: Professional-grade execution
  • Risk-defined strategies: Limit downside while keeping upside potential
  • Flexible expirations: From daily to 3 months forward

How to Deposit (Web)

  1. Go to your Funds screen by clicking the menu dropdown and select "Deposit Add funds"
  2. Click the "Deposit" button next to the asset you want to deposit
  3. Your PowerTrade wallet address will be revealed (QR code available)
  4. Send funds from your external wallet to this address
  5. Wait for blockchain confirmation
  6. Receive email confirmation when funds are credited

⚠️ Important Warning

Make sure to only transfer the correct type of asset into the wallet. Transferring the wrong type of cryptocurrency may render it unrecoverable!

Common Deposit Delays

  • Transaction not broadcast: Check your transaction on the blockchain explorer
  • Network congestion: High traffic can delay processing
  • Low fees: Transactions with higher fees get priority
  • Prior unconfirmed transactions: Wait for previous transactions to confirm
