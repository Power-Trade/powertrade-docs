Options Trading

Crypto options give you the right, but not obligation, to buy or sell cryptocurrency at a fixed price.

  • Call Options: Profit when prices rise
  • Put Options: Profit when prices fall or hedge downside
  • European Style: Exercise only at expiration
  • USDC Settled: All settlements in stable USDC
